Human-Centered Design Tools

IoT4Fun Design Tools follow a HCD perspective. Our HCD tools intervene from inspiration to idea generation, data collection planning, and both low and high-fidelity prototyping.

Context of Use

The HCD proccess starts with understanding what are the user's needs and what are the available resources and design opportunities.

Ideation

Using traditional toys and game rules as resources, the teams can generate innovative ideas.

Low-fidelity

Before moving to functional implementation, the teams must first plan data collection aspects and prototype!

Rapid Prototyping

Our integrated hardware-software technology offers custom and embedded components that fit any smart play solution.

Playtesting

Assessing end-user data is crucial to improve the prototypes. HCD tools make playtesting more agile and effective.

Data Analysis

Data analysis support creating smart play solutions that embrace all stakeholders' needs.

Design Service

Our design services will lead your interdisciplinary team towards a safer product development that meet privacy and quality standards.

Co-criation

When working together, we can drive creations that will inspire users and fulfil all stakeholders' needs.
