Dr. Anna P A Wheler developed a novel collection of Human-Centered Design tools supporting interdisciplinary teams in creating, prototyping, and testing XR solutions for children and family. You can download the digital HCD tools clicking here.

Human-Centered Design Tools

The HCD tools intervene from inspiration to idea generation, data collection planning, and low to high-fidelity prototyping.

Context of Use

The HCD cycle starts understanding who are the stakeholders and their needs, turning problems into design opportunities.

Ideation

The teams generate innovative XR ideas combining visual and tangible resources, making ideas collective and interdisciplinary.

Prototyping

Before jumping to implementation, the teams plan data collection aspects following privacy by design principles.

IoT4Fun Toolkit

Using IoT4Fun XR technology, the teams customize and embedded components that best fit their solutions.

User Research

Assessing end-user data is crucial to improve XR solutions. The HCD tools support remote and in-person user research.

Redesign

The HCD tools support assessing existing XR solutions and improving them with the stakeholders' input.

Training

The HCD tools training leads interdisciplinary team towards a safer product development that meet privacy and quality standards.

Co-criation

When working together, we can deploy inspiring and innovative XR solutions, unlocking novel human potential through technology.
