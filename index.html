IoT4Fun is a Research & Development project focused on defining theoretical and practical tools that will assist companies and professionals in creating Connected Toys and Hybrid Games.

IoT4Fun Toolbox

Our design service will guide your team to working with hybrid design methods and exploring the best from IoT4Fun hardware-software technology.

Conception

We make use of a validated HCI modelling approach to training the teams with hybrid design concepts.

Ideation

Using physical toys as a resource, we can generate innovative ideas to create meaningful play experiences.

Low-fidelity

Before moving to functional aspects, first, we must refine concepts and validate the playful interactions.

Prototyping

Using the integrated hardware-software technology, one can customise & embed the components and program their behaviours.

Playtesting

Testing playable versions are a significant step in the refinement process and hybrid design present new challenges to assessing end-user data.

Evaluation

The HCI research team make use of appropriate tools & methods to analyse and then evaluate hybrid play behaviours.

Consultancy

The consultancy service will lead both the creative & product quality teams in achieving better solutions.

Co-criation

When working together, we can drive creations that will inspire users and fulfil the stakeholder's needs.
